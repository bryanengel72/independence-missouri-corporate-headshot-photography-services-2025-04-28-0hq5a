Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear for my corporate headshot?

We recommend solid colors and professional attire that aligns with your industry. During your consultation, we'll provide detailed guidance based on your specific needs and brand identity.

How long does a session typically last?

Standard sessions run 30-45 minutes, including time for multiple poses and expressions. Executive sessions can extend to 60 minutes for additional looks and background options.

When will I receive my final images?

You'll receive a digital gallery of proofs within 48 hours. After selecting your favorites, final retouched images are delivered within 3-5 business days.

Do you offer group corporate sessions?

Yes! We specialize in corporate team photos and can accommodate groups of any size. We offer special rates and scheduling for corporate clients.

Can I purchase additional retouched photos?

Absolutely! Additional retouched photos can be purchased individually or in packages. We'll provide pricing details during your consultation.
